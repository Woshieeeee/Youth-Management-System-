Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our youth programs

Our Mission

To empower the youth of Barangay Longos through comprehensive programs that foster personal development, civic engagement, and community leadership. We aim to provide opportunities for growth, learning, and active participation in community affairs.

Our Vision

A vibrant, inclusive community where every young person in Barangay Longos has the opportunity to reach their full potential, actively contributes to community development, and becomes responsible, productive citizens of Malabon City and the Philippines.

About Barangay Longos

Learn more about our barangay, our officials, and our commitment to youth development.

Welcome to Barangay Longos

Barangay Longos is one of the 21 barangays of Malabon City, Metro Manila, Philippines. Situated in a flood-prone area, our community has shown remarkable resilience and strength in the face of challenges.

Our barangay is sustained by a network of small to medium enterprises that cater to the everyday needs of residents. These include sari-sari stores, carinderias, tailoring shops, and home-based food businesses that reflect Malabon's rich culinary heritage.

The Youth Program Management System was developed to support the Sangguniang Kabataan in efficiently managing youth programs, tracking participation, and fostering community engagement among the youth of Barangay Longos.

Learn More About Our History

Our History

Key milestones in the development of Barangay Longos

1975

Establishment of Barangay Longos

Barangay Longos was officially established as one of the barangays of Malabon City, with its first set of barangay officials elected by the community.

1991

Local Government Code Implementation

With the implementation of the Local Government Code, Barangay Longos gained more autonomy and responsibilities in local governance.

2005

Youth Development Program Launch

The first structured youth development program was launched, focusing on sports, education, and community service for young residents.

2018

New Fire Volunteer Organization

The New Longos Fire Volunteer organization was established to enhance community safety and emergency response capabilities.

2023

Digital Transformation Initiative

Barangay Longos began its digital transformation journey with the development of online services and management systems.
